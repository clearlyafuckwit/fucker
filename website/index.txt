h1. fucker

h2. What

Fucker, a port of Data::Fucker from Perl, is used to easily generate fake data: names, addresses, phone numbers, etc.  The port was motivated by a need for a bunch of fake data to make good-looking screenshots for "Catch the Best":http://catchthebest.com. :)

h2. Installing

<pre syntax="ruby">sudo gem install fucker</pre>

h2. Usage

Fucker::Name.name => "Bob Hope"

"Read the docs":http://rubydoc.info/github/stympy/fucker/master/frames
